    What should I wear?

    Clothing recommendations adapt to the current UV level so the advice feels useful, not generic.

    Everyday sun-smart outfit

      Quick rules

      • UPF-rated or tightly woven fabrics block more UV than thin cotton.
      • Long sleeves help, but colour, weave, and fit matter too.
      • A hat that shades the face, ears, and neck is much better than a cap.
      • Sunglasses and sunscreen should complement clothing, not replace it.
