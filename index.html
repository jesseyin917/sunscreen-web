    US3.3 · Must Have

    Select the right clothing for the UV level

    Depending on the UV index, users get outfit guidance that balances comfort, coverage, and practical protection.

    Recommended outfit

    Sun-smart outfit guide

      Pick better materials
      • Tightly woven fabrics block more UV than thin or see-through materials.
      • UPF-rated clothing is ideal when outdoor exposure is long.
      • Loose, breathable long sleeves can still feel cool while improving coverage.
      Do not rely on this alone
      • A cap is weaker than a wide-brim hat because ears and neck stay exposed.
      • Sunscreen should support clothing, not replace it.
      • Sunglasses matter too — eye comfort is part of sun safety.
      Campaign message

      Hot fit. Smart fit.

      Good sun protection does not have to look clinical. The goal is to make safer choices feel normal, wearable, and social.
